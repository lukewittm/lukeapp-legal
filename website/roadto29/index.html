About the Event

Movement brings us together

Road to 29 is more than just a run – it's a day where movement, good vibes, and community take center stage. Whether you walk, jog, or roll your way to the finish line – on April 25th we start together and we finish together.

Get ready for a spring day packed with fun, laughter, and good energy!
